St. Mary’s Higher Secondary School, Kaliyar, is a Kerala Government Aided Higher Secondary Institution under the Management of the Diocese of Kothamangalam. The School offers Co Education to students from class One to Twelve in the Kerala State Syllabus. The School is situated in the scenic atmosphere of breathtaking mountains, perennial River Kaliyar and at the heart of Harrison Malayalam rubber the plantations. The School offers Quality Education with the support of dedicated and well qualified staff. The School stands out in the academic sphere of the State with Excellent Results The School’s Principal is Sri. Biju Joseph and Head Mistress is Smt. Sinimol Jose The School’s Manager is Rev Fr.
